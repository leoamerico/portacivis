ADR-ID: ADR-PORTACIVIS-BRAND-GOVERNANCE

Title:
Brand Governance for PortaCivis

Status:
Accepted

Context:
The EnvNeo ecosystem maintains multiple brands
(Govevia, PortaCivis, Env Live). Brand artifacts must be
governed as versioned assets to guarantee consistency
across portals, services and APIs.

Decision:
PortaCivis brand assets will be treated as governed artifacts
with catalog registration, compliance enforcement and
token-based distribution to frontend systems.

Consequences:
Brand assets become part of the governance layer.
All portals must consume brand tokens from the catalog.
